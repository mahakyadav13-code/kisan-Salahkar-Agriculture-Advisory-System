Common Crop Diseases in India

Quick reference guide

Blast (Rice/Wheat)

Diamond-shaped brown spots on leaves. Use Tricyclazole fungicide. Avoid excess nitrogen. Resistant varieties: Pusa-1121, IR-64.

Late Blight (Potato/Tomato)

Dark water-soaked spots on leaves & stems. Spray Mancozeb before monsoon. Use certified disease-free seeds.

Bollworm (Cotton)

Bores into cotton bolls causing yield loss. Use Bt cotton varieties. Install pheromone traps. Spray NPV solution for organic control.

Wilt (Multiple Crops)

Plants suddenly droop despite watering. Caused by Fusarium fungus. Treat soil with Trichoderma. Rotate crops every season.
