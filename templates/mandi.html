Market Insights for Farmers

MSP vs Mandi

Always compare your mandi price with MSP. If mandi price is below MSP, you can sell directly to government procurement centers.

Storage Tips

If prices are low, consider storing grain in a government warehouse (under WDRA scheme) and wait for better prices. Storage cost is ₹4-6/quintal/month.

e-NAM Trading

Register on e-NAM portal to sell crops to buyers across India — get better prices through competitive bidding.
