All Government Schemes

How to Apply: Most schemes can be applied online via pmkisan.gov.in or DBT Agriculture Portal. You'll need Aadhaar, bank details, and land records (khasra/khatauni).

Step-by-Step Application Guide

1
Gather Documents

Aadhaar card, bank passbook, land records (khatauni), caste certificate (if applicable)

2
Visit CSC/Portal

Go to Common Service Center or apply online at pmkisan.gov.in / state agriculture portal

3
Fill Application

Enter personal, land, bank details. Upload documents. Verify with Aadhaar OTP

4
Track Status

Check application status online. Benefits transferred directly to bank via DBT

PM-KISAN

₹6,000/year in 3 installments. All landholding families eligible. Next installment usually in April, August, December.

PMFBY (Fasal Bima)

Crop insurance at 2% premium (Kharif), 1.5% (Rabi). Enroll before sowing deadline through bank or CSC.

Kisan Credit Card

Short-term loan up to ₹3 lakh at 4% interest (with subsidy). Apply at any bank with land documents.
