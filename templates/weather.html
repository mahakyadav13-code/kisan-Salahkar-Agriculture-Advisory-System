Seasonal Weather Guide for Farmers

Monsoon (Jun-Sep)

Heavy rainfall ideal for Kharif crops. Ensure proper drainage, watch for waterlogging, and apply fertilizers before rain.

Winter (Nov-Feb)

Perfect for Rabi crops like wheat & mustard. Protect crops from frost, irrigate in morning hours only.

Summer (Mar-May)

High evaporation — use mulching to retain moisture. Best for Zaid crops like watermelon & cucumber.
